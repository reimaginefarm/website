The Green Wall is a novel approach to vertical soilless farming

Our solution uses the cutting edge technologies like fogponics and the Internet of Things to increase the efficiency and the crop yield. The system saves water and energy by utilizing fog to deliver nutrients and water to the plants. It eliminates usage of water pumps and any other additional noisy & high power consuming systems. Plants planted in the system germinate and grow significantly faster by using less water and electricity.
